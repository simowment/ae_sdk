# Create DropShipping APP

# I. Register as a developer

To register as a developer, you need to meet the following conditions:

1. Have an aliepxpress.com website account After meeting the above conditions, you can register as a developer on the  AE-[Openplatform](https://openservice.aliexpress.com/) .

# 2ã€Register APP

After registering as a developer, you can log in to your developer account on the OpenService platform and enter "App Console" to create your own App. When creating the App, please select the App type as Drop Shipping.

After the App registration is successful, you can find the appkey and appSecret of this App in App Managerment -> Advanced Information.
